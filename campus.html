Campus

Buildings

All program talks will take place on the Queen's University campus. The campus is located in the heart of Kingston and is within walking distance of many hotels, restaurants, and shops. Program talks will take place in Chernoff Hall, Stirling Hall, and the Biosciences Complex.

  • BioSciences Complex

    116 Barrie St, Kingston, ON K7L 3N6

  • Chernoff Hall

    90 Bader Ln, Kingston, ON K7L 2S8

  • Stirling Hall

    64 Bader Ln, Kingston, ON K7L 3N6
